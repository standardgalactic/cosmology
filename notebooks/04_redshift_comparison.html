RSVP Notebook 4 — Redshift Without Dark Energy

Luminosity distance d_L(z): comparing ΛCDM, ATV, and RSVP through the third-order coefficient C

The Observable

The expansion history is encoded in the luminosity–redshift relation:

H₀d_L = z + Qz² + Cz³ + O(z⁴)

The coefficient C discriminates between theories. ΛCDM predicts C<0; ATV and RSVP predict C>0.

Notes

The three theories are degenerate at first order (H₀) and nearly degenerate at second order (Q ≈ 0.42–0.5 for all). The discriminating signal is entirely at third order in z.
